My Project. In detail

Project name:
OurUs
App

OurUs is a family space where members will have access (depending on the permissions that are assigned) to all information regarding children, a calendar where they can assign tasks to each component and if necessary with which child has to perform them.

Interact with the low fidelity prototype

They will also have an agenda of family importance and chat among them. Once the user completes the task and confirms it, the rest of the users will be able to see its update. In this way, all the people involved in the care of the children will be able to have in one place all the information referring to them. It includes the possibility of giving temporary access and defining user roles. In addition, in the section "Have fun" you can have access to different ways to entertain the little ones, from stories to games adapted to the age of (individually or together) of the children.
